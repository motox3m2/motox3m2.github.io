Moto X3M is a free dirt bike racing game where you have to ride a cool motorcycle on an obstacle course. The great motocross racing game takes you to the cold snowy mountains. You have to prove you're the best dirt bike rider ever. Race as fast as you can to score points. Make flips and try to master all off-road circuits in best time in the cool online game Moto X3M 2.

Face up the winter challenge and have fun. New dangerous traps and obstacles as well as new upgrades for your bike are waiting for you in this online game. Try to keep the balance on an uneven terrain and don't fell off the track. Perform as many flips in teh air as possible. Every stunt will save you some seconds of the time. The faster you get to the finish line the more stars you get.

INSTRUCTIONS: Arrows = drive / balanceSpacebar = respawn at last checkpoint
